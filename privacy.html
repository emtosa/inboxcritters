Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Inbox Critters: Brain Dump ("the App") is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Your brain dumps stay exactly where they belong: on your device.

Data We Collect

None. The App does not collect any personal information whatsoever. This includes:

  • No name, email address, or account information
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports sent to external servers
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contacts or other device data

Local Storage

Any data you enter in the App — your thoughts, priorities, and session results — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's standard local storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or external APIs. There are no trackers of any kind embedded in the App.

Internet Connection

The App does not require an internet connection and does not make any network requests. It works fully offline, always.

Children's Privacy

Because we collect no data from anyone, there are no special considerations for users under the age of 13. The App is safe for all ages from a data privacy perspective.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, if changes are significant, notify users through the App Store update notes.

Contact

Questions about privacy? You can reach us through the App Store listing:

https://apps.apple.com/app/id6759697101
