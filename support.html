Support & FAQ

Answers to common questions about Inbox Critters: Brain Dump.

How do I do a brain dump?

Open the app and type every thought swirling in your head — one per line. Don't filter or edit. Just get it out as fast as you can. When you tap Done, each line becomes a floating thought orb.

What are the 4 priority buckets?

MIT (Most Important Today — do this first), High (important, do next), Normal (on the list, no rush), and Someday (captured so your brain can let it go). Drag each orb into the right bucket.

What are the critters trying to do?

Mischievous critters scuttle across the screen and try to drag unprocessed thought orbs away. It adds a fun urgency to the triage process. Don't worry — you can retrieve stolen orbs by tapping them.

What happens after I sort everything?

You'll see a summary of your priorities. Your MIT tasks are highlighted at the top, ready for your focus session. The sorted list saves locally on your device.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, completely. Inbox Critters works with no internet connection. Everything stays on your device.

Does it collect any data?

No. 100% local. No accounts, no analytics, no ads.

Still need help?

Contact us through the App Store listing and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
